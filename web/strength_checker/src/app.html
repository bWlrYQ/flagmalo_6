# Installation (for UNIX systems)

## Step 1:
wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bWlrYQ/StrengthChecker/main/strengthChecker.py -o strengthChecker.py

## Step 2:
chmod +x strengthChecker.py

# How To Use

## Step 1:
./strengthChecker.py --password {YourPasswordHere}

## Step 2:
If the programme tells you your password is secure keep it up, if ever it tells you it is not, change your password immediately !
